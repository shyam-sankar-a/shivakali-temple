Getting Here

How to Reach

🚂
By Train

Haripad Railway Station — 1 km from the temple. Trains from Thiruvananthapuram (1.5 hrs) and Ernakulam (2 hrs).

🚌
By Bus

KSRTC and private buses connect Haripad to Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi, and Alappuzha. Regular services throughout the day.

🚗
By Car / Taxi

Located on the NH-66 coastal highway. Ample parking available near the temple premises.
